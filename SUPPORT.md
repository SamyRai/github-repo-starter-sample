# Support

Support channels:

- Bug reports and feature requests: open an **Issue** using the templates in `.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/`

- Questions and discussion: use the **Discussions** tab or email
  [support@glpx.pro](mailto:support@glpx.pro)

- Priority or security issues: follow `SECURITY.md` and contact
  [security@glpx.pro](mailto:security@glpx.pro)

Response expectations:

- Triage: within 3 business days

- Standard support reply: within 7 business days

- Critical/security: prioritized and handled through `SECURITY.md`

Provide as much detail as possible (steps to reproduce, logs, environment)
 to speed triage.
