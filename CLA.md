# Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

This repository requires contributors to sign a CLA before their changes can be
merged.

How to sign:

- Follow the CLA Assistant flow when prompted by your first PR: [CLA Assistant](https://cla-assistant.io)

- If you cannot sign online, contact [legal@glpx.pro](mailto:legal@glpx.pro) for alternatives.

Questions about the CLA or exceptions should be directed to
[legal@glpx.pro](mailto:legal@glpx.pro).
